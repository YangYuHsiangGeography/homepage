2016-1 IYGU Story Map : China house-buying power is rolling the world up

Chinese-ethnic people buy foreign real estate crazily become an irrepressible trend, simultaneously, Chinese-ethnic local society is undergoing an impact of high housing price. Plenty of young people are unable to afford a decent house even if they work all their lives. Accordingly, they can only rent a house or live with parents like parasite or live in the small and shabby room like a snail .

At the end of story, we will learn “Ratio of house price to income” in Chinese-ethnic cities which is so high that represents an living problem for local residents. Looking in the problem in detail, government’s economic policies and social housing policies effect this issue intensely. What exactly happen to all of this ? Let’s begin our adventure of comprehension.
